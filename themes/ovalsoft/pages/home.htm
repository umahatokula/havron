Welcome To Havron

Havron Professional and Educational Services is a registered limited liability company in Nigeria. We are an eLearning hub for educational, professional and career development purposes.

There are palpable desires for academic pursuit, professional development and career growth yearning for fulfilment. This huge rift between desire and fulfilment is predicated on two factors: inadequate funds and time for physical classroom sessions; these need to be addressed.

We see eLearning as the opportunity to bridge this rift. We achieve this by intermediating between candidates and securing affordable and quality education for the underserved. These can be in form of full or partial scholarships with suitable and flexible payment plans.

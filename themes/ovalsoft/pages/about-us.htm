About us

Our MISSION

To use the strength in number in scaling down cost of tuitions and facilitate access to affordable online education and professional development programs to the underserved.

Our VISION

To be a leading eLearning hub in Africa for academic, professional and career development.

Our STRATEGY

To use our social media platform and capacity building arm, Havron Professional Development Group (HPDG) to recruit members and participants into our growing social networks. In our social platforms, we share details of available courses from our partners and recruit suitably qualified candidates into available programs.

Our membership spans across all disciplines and has grown well over 800 followers. Our members work in development sector, government and private hospitals, MDAs and government parastatals.
